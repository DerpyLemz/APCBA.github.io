APCBA’s Branches and Other Programs and Services

Asia Pacific College of Business and Arts has two branches: main branch located at Manzano St., Poblacion Norte, Rizal, Nueva Ecija, and second branch located at The Lord’s Acre, Larcon, Bongabon, Nueva Ecija.

APCBA Rizal branch provides training and assessment for Events Management Services NC III, Housekeeping NC II, Hilot (Wellness Massage) NC II, Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC II, and Pipefitting (Metallic) NC II, while Bongabon branch is a training and assessment center for Agricultural Crops Production NC II.

“Asia Pacific College of Business and Arts continues to expand and add its offered programs as well as develop its facilities and resources to better its performance and service in accommodating and catering the diverse educational needs of its students and every person who dreams to be an Asianite,” said Ma’am Eden B. Olivar, officer-in-charge of the school.
